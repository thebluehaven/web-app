Our Story

"Blue Haven is a solution that has seen its inception to solve the commonly faced problem of the citizens in Bangalore – to be able to find a comfortable home without having to pay huge lump sum of money towards brokerage. Blue Haven emphasises strictly on the concept of ‘NON-BROKERAGE’. It only relies on the dealings between the client and the company’s and not a single penny is charged further. To facilitate a home-like ambience, Blue Haven allows customisation of the home space with lavish furnishing and decor. The homes and the flats provide a safe, secure and an enriching experience. Special facilities such as hard water filter, CCTV surveillance and power backup are also provided. And all of this at affordable pricing!"
